SecondaryClOrdID (526)

Unique identifier for Order as assigned by the exchange, ECN or system. Uniqueness should be guaranteed within a single trading day (best practice = embedding a date within the OrderID field to assure uniqueness across days).
Assigned by the Broker or firm which originated the order. Used to propagate the ClOrdID (11) used by the exchange, ECN or system.

Type: String

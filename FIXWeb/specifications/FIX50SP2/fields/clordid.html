ClOrdID (11)

Assigned by the Broker or firm which originated the order. Used to propagate the ClOrdID (11) used by the exchange, ECN or system.
For Fills of no-last-look Quotes, this message will refer to the Executed Quote. Mapped to:
- QuoteMsgID(1166) of a single Quote
- QuoteID(117) of a Mass Quote.

Type: String

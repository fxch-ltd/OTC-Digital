ExpireTime (126)

Time/Date of expiration (always expressed in UTC (Universal Time Coordinated, also known as "GMT")
For Quotes - this is the expiration of the quote.
Expiration time is provided across the quote message dialog to control the length of time of the overall quoting process.

Type: UTCTimestamp

Used in Messages:

[QuoteStatusReport] 

Used in Components:
